About Maragoli Hills

The Maragoli Hills are a prominent feature of the Vihiga landscape, offering breathtaking views and excellent hiking opportunities. The hills are steeped in the culture and history of the Maragoli people. A hike to the top provides a rewarding experience with panoramic vistas of the surrounding green countryside, making it a favorite spot for both locals and tourists seeking a peaceful escape into nature and a bit of exercise.
