A Hiker's Paradise: Exploring the Maragoli Hills

Explore the scenic hiking trails and stunning panoramic views of the beautiful Maragoli Hills in Vihiga County.

Discover the Beauty of Vihiga County

The Maragoli Hills offer some of the most breathtaking panoramic views in Western Kenya. A hike through these scenic landscapes is a must-do for any nature lover.

What to Expect

  • Winding trails suitable for all fitness levels.
  • Incredible opportunities for photography.
  • A chance to connect with local culture and history.

Plan your day trip from Broadpark Hotels and experience the tranquility of the hills.
