Masinde Muliro Gardens: An Urban Oasis

Find peace and tranquility at the Masinde Muliro Gardens, a beautiful botanical park perfect for family outings.

A Peaceful Escape in Kakamega Town

The Masinde Muliro Gardens offer a beautiful and tranquil space for leisurely walks and family outings. Admire the diverse plant species and the iconic statue.

Perfect For:

  • A relaxing afternoon stroll.
  • Family picnics in a serene environment.
  • Learning about local botanical diversity.

A wonderful and easily accessible attraction for guests of all ages.
