The Legend of the Crying Stone of Ilesi

Uncover the mystery and cultural significance of the Crying Stone of Ilesi, a unique geological wonder.

A Wonder Steeped in Folklore

The Crying Stone of Ilesi is one of the most intriguing natural monuments in the region. This massive rock formation appears to weep, a phenomenon tied to ancient local legends.

Why You Should Visit

  • Learn about the rich cultural history and folklore.
  • Enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding countryside.
  • A unique and memorable photo opportunity.

Ask our front desk about arranging a guided tour to this mystical site.
